About A(DAI)

A(DAI) is a practice-led infrastructure project for the digital arts. We consolidate collective intelligence from artists, curators, developers, and researchers — building the narrative and technical layer the emergent digital arts ecosystem needs.

The institute is not a single authority. It is a commons. Not 'the' digital arts institute, but 'a' — one that is forkable, recomposable, and open by design.
